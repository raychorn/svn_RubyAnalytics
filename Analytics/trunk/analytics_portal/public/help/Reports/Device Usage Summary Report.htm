Device Usage Summary Report

The Device Usage Summary Report displays a summary of important information about each of your devices in a tabular format. The Device Usage Summary appears on the Reports Screen under the Customer Profiling category.

Type

Tabular

Parameters

●  Time Range – default to past week

●  Default sort by Share descending

Columns

●  Manufacturer

●  Device Model

●  Share – Calculate the percent of total users with this device.

●  Time Connected/day – Calculate average time connected per day of users with this device.

●  Throughput/day – Calculate average data uploaded and downloaded per day of users with this device.

Sample

Sorting

●  Manufacturer ASC, Device ASC

●  Device ASC

●  Share DEC, Time Conn/day DEC, Throughput/day DEC

●  Time Conn/day DEC, Share DEC, Throughput/day DEC

●  Throughput/day, Share DEC, Throughput/day DEC
