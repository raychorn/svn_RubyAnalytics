Device Usage Pie Chart

The Device Usage Pie Chart allows you to see in relation to each other, which devices your users are using. The Device Usage Pie Chart appears on the Reports Screen under the Customer Profiling category.

Type

Pie Chart

Parameters

●  Time Range – default to past week

Fields

●  Share – Calculate the percent of total users with this device.

Segment Tooltip

When the user hovers over a pie segment,a callout appears showing:

●  Manufacturer

●  Device Model

●  Percentage
