Licensing Details Report

Customer Profiling > Licensing Details

Type

Tabular

Parameters

●  Time Range – default to past year

●  Manufacturer (Data.161)

●  Device Model (Data.162)

●  Default sort by Month ASC (no other sorting)

Fields

●  Month – Month and year

●  Returning – Number of returning – from any previous month – serial numbers (DataParam.141)

●  New - Number of new – never seen before – serial numbers (DataParam.141)

●  Lost – Number of serial numbers present in previous month that did not return (DataParam.141)

●  Total – Returning + New

●  Net Gain/Loss – New - Lost

●  Churn – Lost / Previous Month’s Total * 100

●  Year over Year - % change in total active serial numbers from 1 year previous. (Total – Previous Year Total)/Previous Year Total * 100
