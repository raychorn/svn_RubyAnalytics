Licensing Report

The Licensing Report Report appears on the Reports Screen under the Customer Profiling category.

Type

Mixed – stacked bar and line graph

Parameters

●  Time Range – default to past year

●  Manufacturer

●  Device Model

Data

X-axis

●  Time, show past year.

Y-axis

●  Stacked Bar graph – Number of new – never seen before – serial numbers

●  Stacked Bar graph – Number of returning – from any previous month – serial numbers

●  Stacked Bar graph – Number of serial numbers present in previous month that did not return

●  Line graph – Number of lost serial numbers divided by the previous month’s total active serial numbers
