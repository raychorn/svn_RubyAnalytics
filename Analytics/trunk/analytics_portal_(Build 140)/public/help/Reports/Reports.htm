Reports

The Smith Micro Analytics Portal has a number of predefined reports that you can choose from to view different aspects of your data. The reports are divided into to three main categories:

●  Customer Profiling — These reports provide insights into your customers' activities including data and device usage and licensing churn.

●  Error Management — These reports help you to pinpoint errors that are occurring in your network.

●  Network Performance —

An additional report, the Key Performance Indicators report, appears under the "My Reports" category.
