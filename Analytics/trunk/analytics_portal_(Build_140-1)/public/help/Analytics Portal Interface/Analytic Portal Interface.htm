Analytic Portal Interface

The Analytics Portal Interface consists of a variety of screens. Each screen is divided into two areas: the Navigation Area and the Content Area.

When the Analytics Portal is first opened, the first screen displayed is the Dashboard screen. The Content Area of the Dashboard Screen screen provides a sampling of the reports that are available from the Analytics Portal. In addition to the Dashboard screen, there are two other main screens: the Reports and the Data Segments screens. The Content Area of the Reports Screen screen allows you to select individual reports to view, to change parameters on those reports, and to export the reports to other file formats. The Content Area of the Data Segments Screen screen allows you to view the status of and to maintain data segments.
