Average Signal Strength Heat Map Report

The Average Signal Strength Heat Map shows you the average signal strength during a specified time range as an overlay on a map. The darker the block, the greater the average signal strength in that area. The Average Signal Strength Heat Map appears on the Reports screen under the Network Performance category.

Type

Map

Parameters

●  Time Range – default to past month

●  Connection Type

Data

Display heat map of average signal strength. Include base station markers on map.
