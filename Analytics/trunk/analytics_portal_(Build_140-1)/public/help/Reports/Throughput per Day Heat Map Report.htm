Throughput per Day Heat Map Report

The Throughput per Day Heat Map shows you the average volume of daily data traffic as an overlay on a map. The darker the block, the greater the average volume of daily data traffic in that area. The Throughput per Day Heat Map appears on the Reports screen under the Network Performance category.

Type

Map

Parameters

●  Time Range – default to past month

●  Connection Type

Data

Display heat map of sum of average data uploaded and downloaded per day. Include base station markers on map.
